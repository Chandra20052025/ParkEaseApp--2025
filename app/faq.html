Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a parking slot?

Log in to your ParkEase account, go to the dashboard and select "Slot Booking". Choose Car or Bike, pick your date and time, select an available slot, then click "Book Slot". You'll be redirected to complete payment.


Can I borrow a parking slot from a friend?

Yes! Go to the "Friends List" page, find your friend, and click "Request". Fill in the date, time, and vehicle type. Your friend will receive a notification and can approve or deny the request.


How can I view my booking history?

From the dashboard, click "Booking History". This shows all your past and upcoming bookings with their status — Confirmed, Active, Expired, or Cancelled.


How do I cancel a booking?

Go to "Booking History", find your confirmed booking, and click the "Cancel" button. Cancellation is only available for future bookings that haven't started yet.


How do I add friends?

Go to the "Friends List" page and search for a user by their name. Click "Add to Friends" to add them to your list. You can then send them parking slot requests.


How do I update my profile?

Click the menu icon (☰) in the top right, then select "Edit Profile". Update your name, vehicle number, license number, or mobile number and click "Save Changes".
