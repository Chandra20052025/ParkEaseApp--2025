Privacy Policy

Introduction

At ParkEase, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

Data We Collect

We collect information you provide during registration, including your name, email address, phone number, vehicle number, and driving license number. We also collect booking and usage data to improve our services.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to manage your account, process bookings, send notifications, and improve our platform. We do not sell your personal information to third parties under any circumstances.

Data Sharing

We may share limited data with trusted service providers (such as payment processors) strictly to operate our services. All third parties are contractually obligated to keep your data secure.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption and secure authentication to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time via the Edit Profile page. To request full data deletion, please contact our support team.

Cookies

ParkEase may use session cookies to maintain your login state. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of ParkEase after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us at support@parkease.com.
